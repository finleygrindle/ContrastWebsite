About

Founded in 2021, Green Bean is a nonprofit organisation with a passion for our earth. We are working daily to combat food waste in the local area, with aspirations to reach a global scale. Our restaurant is pay as you feel, featuring cost efficient daily menus consisting of diverted fresh food from local supermarkets and distributors. We are targeted towards students and young families in need of a well-priced, healthy meal. Despite this, our door is always open to anyone with a devotion for saving our planet!
